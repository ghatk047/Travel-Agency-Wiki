CX-CS-03

Booking Dispute and Complaint Resolution

This process document outlines the steps for resolving booking disputes and complaints between customers and travel technology experts at Expedia Group (OTA) and Amex GBT / SAP Concur (TMC).

CX — OTA Customer Experience and Service Customer Service Operations

Process Overview

Trigger A customer initiates a dispute or complaint through various channels such as email, phone, chat, or social media.
Outcome The issue is resolved to the satisfaction of the customer, with appropriate compensation or adjustments made to their booking. The process ensures that all parties are informed and records are updated accordingly.
