CX-PX-05

Localisation and Multi-Currency Display

This process document outlines the localisation and multi-currency display functionality for OTA and TMC systems, ensuring a seamless user experience across different regions and currencies.

CX — OTA Customer Experience and Service Personalisation and User Experience

Process Overview

Trigger User initiates a search or booking request on an Expedia Group platform or submits an expense report through Amex GBT / Concur.
Outcome The system successfully displays prices in the user's local currency and language, providing a personalized and localized experience.
Systems Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau Concur T2 Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete Amadeus GDS S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday
