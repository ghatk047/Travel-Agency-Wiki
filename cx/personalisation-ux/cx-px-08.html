CX-PX-08

Customer Data Platform CDP Management

This process document outlines the management of a Customer Data Platform (CDP) for Expedia Group's OTA and TMC systems, focusing on personalization and user experience improvements.

CX — OTA Customer Experience and Service Personalisation and User Experience

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new CDP project or update to an existing one based on business needs or technological advancements.
Outcome A robust CDP system that integrates seamlessly with all relevant OTA and TMC systems, enabling personalized customer experiences and improved service delivery.
Systems AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds Concur T2 Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday
