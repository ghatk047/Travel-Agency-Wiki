DC-CR-04

GDPR Data Privacy Compliance Management

This process document outlines the GDPR Data Privacy Compliance Management for TMC Duty of Care and Risk Management within Expedia Group and Amex GBT / SAP Concur.

DC — TMC Duty of Care and Risk Management Compliance and Regulatory

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new data privacy compliance requirement or identification of non-compliance issues.
Outcome Successful implementation and maintenance of GDPR compliance across all relevant systems, ensuring data protection and privacy for users.
