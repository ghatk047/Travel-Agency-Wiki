DC-CR-06

Anti-Bribery and FCPA Travel Compliance

This process document outlines the steps for ensuring Anti-Bribery and FCPA compliance during travel activities managed by Expedia Group's OTA systems and Amex GBT / SAP Concur (TMC) systems.

DC — TMC Duty of Care and Risk Management Compliance and Regulatory

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new travel request or modification to an existing travel plan involving international travel or high-risk destinations.
Outcome Successful completion of the Anti-Bribery and FCPA compliance checks, ensuring all travel activities are in adherence with relevant laws and regulations.
