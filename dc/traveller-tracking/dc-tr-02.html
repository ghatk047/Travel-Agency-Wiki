Pre-Trip Risk Assessment and Destination Intelligence

This process document outlines the Pre-Trip Risk Assessment and Destination Intelligence for TMC Duty of Care and Risk Management, focusing on traveller tracking and safety.

DC — TMC Duty of Care and Risk Management Traveller Tracking and Safety

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new travel booking or update to an existing booking
Outcome A comprehensive pre-trip risk assessment is completed, providing destination intelligence and safety recommendations to the traveler.
Systems Concur T2 AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Expedia Open World Partner Central
