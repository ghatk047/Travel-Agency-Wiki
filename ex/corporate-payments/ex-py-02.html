EX-PY-02

Virtual Card VCN Lodge Card Issuance and Control

Process for issuing and controlling Virtual Card VCN Lodge Cards through TMC systems.

EX — TMC Expense and Payments Corporate Payments and Settlement

Process Overview

Trigger Request for a new Virtual Card VCN Lodge Card from a corporate user via SAP Concur Expense or Amex GBT Neo/Complete.
Outcome Successful issuance of the Virtual Card VCN Lodge Card with proper control measures in place, ensuring compliance and security.
Systems SAP Concur T2 SAP Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete VCN Amex Corporate Card Salesforce Email System SAP Analytics Cloud Tableau Snowflake AWS SageMaker
