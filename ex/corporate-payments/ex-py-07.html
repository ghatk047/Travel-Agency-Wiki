EX-PY-07

Hotel Direct Bill and Central Billing Management

This process document outlines the steps for managing hotel direct bills and central billing within the TMC Expense and Payments domain, focusing on corporate payments and settlement.

EX — TMC Expense and Payments Corporate Payments and Settlement

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new billing cycle or receipt of a hotel bill
Outcome Successful processing and reconciliation of hotel bills, with accurate payment to hotels and updated financial records in both OTA and TMC systems.
Systems SAP Concur Expense SAP Concur T2 SAP S/4HANA SAP Analytics Cloud
