EX-PY-08

Payment Fraud Detection and Dispute Resolution

This process document outlines the steps for detecting and resolving payment fraud within the TMC Expense and Payments domain, focusing on corporate payments and settlements.

EX — TMC Expense and Payments Corporate Payments and Settlement

Process Overview

Trigger Detection of suspicious activity in transaction data or receipt of a dispute from a cardholder.
Outcome Successful identification and resolution of fraudulent transactions with minimal impact on legitimate business operations.
Systems AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau Concur T2 Concur Expense S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday

Key Performance Indicators

Detection rate of fraudulent transactions Time to investigate suspicious transactions Accuracy of fraud detection Response time to flagged transactions Resolution time for disputes Accuracy of transaction updates Timeliness of fraud reports Improvement in fraud detection accuracy Customer satisfaction with dispute resolution process Alignment of internal policies with fraud prevention best practices

Risks & Exceptions

High volume of transactions leading to delayed investigations Complex transaction patterns making investigation difficult False positives/negatives affecting business operations Communication delays impacting stakeholder awareness Complex dispute resolution processes causing prolonged disputes Data consistency issues leading to incorrect transaction statuses Challenges in data visualization affecting fraud report accuracy Model overfitting/underfitting reducing the effectiveness of fraud detection models Handling sensitive customer information during communication Policy implementation challenges leading to non-compliance
