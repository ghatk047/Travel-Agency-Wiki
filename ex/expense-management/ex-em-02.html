Receipt Capture and OCR Processing AI-powered

This process document outlines the EX-EM-02 Receipt Capture and OCR Processing AI-powered workflow for TMC Expense and Payments.

EX — TMC Expense and Payments Expense Management

Process Overview

Trigger Employee submits a receipt through the SAP Concur Expense or Amex GBT Neo/Complete system.
Outcome The receipt is accurately captured, processed via OCR, and integrated into the respective TMC systems with all relevant data extracted and validated.
Systems SAP Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau
