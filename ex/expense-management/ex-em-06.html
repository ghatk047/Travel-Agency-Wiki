EX-EM-06

ERP Integration and Reimbursement Processing S4HANA

This process document outlines the integration and reimbursement processing between Expedia Group's OTA systems and Amex GBT / Concur (TMC) using S4HANA for expense management.

EX — TMC Expense and Payments Expense Management

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new project or update to existing ERP integration
Outcome Successful integration and seamless reimbursement processing across all systems, ensuring accurate and timely expense reporting and payment.
Systems S4HANA Expedia Group OTA Systems Amex GBT / Concur TMC Systems AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka
