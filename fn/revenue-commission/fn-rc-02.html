Hotel Commission Collection and Dispute Management

This process document outlines the steps for collecting hotel commissions and managing disputes between Expedia Group (OTA) and Amex GBT / SAP Concur (TMC).

FN — Finance and Settlement Revenue and Commission Management

Process Overview

Trigger A new booking is confirmed or an existing booking is modified.
Outcome Hotel commissions are accurately collected, and any disputes are resolved promptly and efficiently.
