FN-RC-04

Virtual Card EVC Issuance and Hotel Settlement

This process document outlines the steps for issuing a Virtual Card (EVC) and settling hotel expenses using systems from Expedia Group (OTA) and Amex GBT / Concur (TMC).

FN — Finance and Settlement Revenue and Commission Management

Process Overview

Trigger A request to issue a Virtual Card and settle hotel expenses is received.
Outcome The Virtual Card is successfully issued, and the hotel expenses are settled without errors.
