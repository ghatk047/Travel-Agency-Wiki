Ancillary Revenue Accounting Insurance Seats Bags

This process document outlines the accounting and revenue management for insurance, seat, and bag ancillary services within the Expedia Group OTA and Amex GBT / Concur TMC systems.

FN — Finance and Settlement Revenue and Commission Management

Process Overview

Trigger The initiation of a new booking or change in an existing booking that includes ancillary services like insurance, seats, or bags.
Outcome Successful accounting and revenue management for ancillary services with accurate financial reporting and commission distribution.
