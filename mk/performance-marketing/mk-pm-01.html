MK-PM-01

Paid Search Google Ads Campaign Management

This process document outlines the management of Paid Search Google Ads campaigns for Expedia Group and Amex GBT / SAP Concur, focusing on optimizing ad performance and driving conversions.

MK — OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty Performance Marketing

Process Overview

Trigger The initiation of a new marketing campaign or the need to adjust an existing one based on performance metrics.
Outcome A successful campaign that meets or exceeds conversion goals, with optimized cost per acquisition (CPA) and return on ad spend (ROAS).
Systems Google Ads Expedia Open World Tableau Snowflake Kafka Salesforce
