MK-PM-02

Metasearch CPC Bid Management Google Hotel Ads Trivago

This process document outlines the Metasearch CPC Bid Management for Google Hotel Ads and Trivago, focusing on optimizing ad performance through bid adjustments based on keyword performance data.

MK — OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty Performance Marketing

Process Overview

Trigger Weekly review of keyword performance metrics from Google Hotel Ads and Trivago platforms.
Outcome Optimized CPC bids leading to improved click-through rates (CTR) and lower cost per conversion (CPC) for hotel bookings.
Systems Google Hotel Ads API Trivago API AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Tableau Salesforce
