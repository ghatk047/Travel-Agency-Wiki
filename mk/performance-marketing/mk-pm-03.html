MK-PM-03

Programmatic Display and Retargeting

This process document outlines the steps for implementing programmatic display and retargeting campaigns across OTA and TMC systems to optimize marketing media spend and enhance loyalty programs.

MK — OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty Performance Marketing

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new marketing campaign or update to an existing one, requiring programmatic display and retargeting strategies.
Outcome Successful execution of programmatic display and retargeting campaigns leading to increased brand awareness, higher conversion rates, and improved customer engagement metrics.
