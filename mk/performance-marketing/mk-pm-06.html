MK-PM-06

App Store Optimisation and Mobile Growth

This process document outlines the App Store Optimization and Mobile Growth strategy for Expedia Group's OTA and TMC systems, focusing on enhancing mobile app visibility and user engagement.

MK — OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty Performance Marketing

Process Overview

Trigger Launch of a new mobile feature or update to existing features in Expedia Open World, Partner Central, Amadeus NDC, Sabre GDS, Travelport, EVC, One Key, TravelAds, AWS SageMaker, Snowflake, Kafka, Salesforce, Tableau for OTA systems and SAP Concur T2, SAP Concur Expense, Amex GBT Neo/Complete, Amadeus GDS, SAP S/4HANA, International SOS, Cvent, Amex Corporate Card, VCN, SAP Analytics Cloud, Workday for TMC systems.
Outcome Increased app downloads, higher user engagement, and improved conversion rates through optimized App Store listings and mobile growth strategies.
