MK-PM-08

Marketing Attribution and ROI Measurement

This process document outlines the steps for Marketing Attribution and ROI Measurement within the OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty domain, focusing on performance marketing.

MK — OTA Marketing Media and Loyalty Performance Marketing

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new marketing campaign or review of existing campaigns
Outcome Accurate attribution of marketing efforts to revenue generation and clear measurement of ROI across various channels and systems.

Key Performance Indicators

Clear definition of success metrics Consistent tracking across platforms Campaign launch within timeline Timely reporting of campaign performance Accurate attribution of marketing efforts to revenue Stakeholder satisfaction with reporting Improved campaign performance post-optimization Continuous improvement of marketing strategies Compliance with data retention policies Comprehensive documentation of campaign outcomes

Risks & Exceptions

Misalignment between marketing goals and business objectives Inaccurate data collection due to tracking setup issues Coordination challenges across multiple platforms Handling large volumes of data efficiently Complexity in data analysis leading to incorrect insights Difficulty in communicating technical data effectively Balancing testing with business needs during optimization Adjusting goals based on dynamic market conditions without losing focus Managing large datasets over time for compliance and efficiency Incomplete documentation of campaign outcomes affecting future strategies
