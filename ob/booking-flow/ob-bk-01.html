OB-BK-01

Booking Initiation and Session Management

This process document outlines the Booking Initiation and Session Management for OTA Online Booking and Search, covering both Expedia Group (OTA) and Amex GBT / Concur (TMC) systems.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Booking Flow and Order Management

Process Overview

Trigger A user initiates a booking request through an OTA platform or TMC system.
Outcome The booking session is successfully initiated, and the user can proceed with searching for and selecting travel options.

Risks & Exceptions

High volumes of concurrent booking requests leading to system overload Data inconsistency across distributed systems during session management Invalid or incomplete travel details causing search failures Integration issues with multiple GDS systems for real-time data Optimization challenges for displaying large datasets in the user interface Handling multiple simultaneous selections without conflicts Ensuring atomicity and consistency of session states across distributed systems Complex validation rules leading to increased processing time Payment failures and retries causing delays in booking confirmation Data synchronization issues between OTA and TMC systems
