OB-BK-04

Payment Processing and Fraud Screening

This process document outlines the payment processing and fraud screening workflow for OTA online booking and search, covering systems like Expedia Open World, Partner Central, Amadeus NDC, Sabre GDS, Travelport, EVC, One Key, TravelAds, AWS SageMaker, Snowflake, Kafka, Salesforce, Tableau, SAP Concur T2, SAP Concur Expense, Amex GBT Neo/Complete, Amadeus GDS, SAP S/4HANA, International SOS, Cvent, Amex Corporate Card, VCN, SAP Analytics Cloud, and Workday.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Booking Flow and Order Management

Process Overview

Trigger A booking is confirmed by the traveler or authorized user within the OTA system.
Outcome Successful payment processing with no fraud detected, allowing the booking to be finalized and travel arrangements to proceed.
