OB-BK-05

Supplier Booking Confirmation and PNR Receipt

This process document outlines the steps involved in confirming a supplier booking and generating a Passenger Name Record (PNR) receipt within the OTA Online Booking and Search domain.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Booking Flow and Order Management

Process Overview

Trigger A customer completes a booking through Expedia Group's online platform or via an API call from a third-party system.
Outcome The process successfully confirms the booking with the supplier, generates a PNR receipt, and updates all relevant systems with the booking details.
