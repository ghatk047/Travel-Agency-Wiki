OB-BK-07

Booking Modification and Amendment Handling

This process document outlines the steps involved in handling booking modifications and amendments for OTA (Online Travel Agency) and TMC (Travel Management Company) systems.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Booking Flow and Order Management

Process Overview

Trigger A request to modify or amend a booking is received from a customer or internal system.
Outcome The booking modification or amendment is successfully processed, and all relevant parties are notified of the changes.
