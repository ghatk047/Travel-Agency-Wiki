OTA White Label and B2B API Management EPS

This process document outlines the management of OTA white label and B2B API services for Expedia Group and Amex GBT / Concur, focusing on integration, monitoring, and optimization.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Channel and Distribution Management

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new OTA or TMC system integration request or an update to existing APIs.
Outcome Successful deployment and management of white label and B2B API services with optimized performance and reliability.
