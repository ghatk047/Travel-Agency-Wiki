OB-CH-05

Metasearch Channel Bidding Google Kayak Trivago

This process document outlines the OB-CH-05 Metasearch Channel Bidding for Google Kayak and Trivago, focusing on optimizing bids to improve booking outcomes through OTA and TMC systems integration.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Channel and Distribution Management

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new bidding strategy or update to existing strategies based on market analysis and performance metrics.
Outcome Successful implementation of the metasearch channel bidding strategy leading to improved conversion rates and revenue for Google Kayak and Trivago channels.
