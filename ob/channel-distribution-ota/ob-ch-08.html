OB-CH-08

Affiliate and Co-Brand Partner Channel Management

This process document outlines the management of affiliate and co-brand partner channels for Expedia Group's OTA and TMC systems, ensuring seamless integration and optimization.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Channel and Distribution Management

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new partnership or update to an existing channel agreement
Outcome Successful onboarding and ongoing management of affiliate and co-brand partners, leading to increased bookings and revenue through optimized distribution channels.
