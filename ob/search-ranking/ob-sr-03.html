OB-SR-03

Car Rental and Activities Search Integration

Integrate car rental and activities search functionalities across OTA and TMC systems to enhance user experience and optimize bookings.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Search Ranking and Personalisation

Process Overview

Trigger User initiates a search for car rentals or activities on Expedia Group platforms.
Outcome Successful integration of car rental and activities search, resulting in improved search ranking, personalization, and seamless booking experiences for users.
Systems Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau SAP Concur T2 SAP Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete SAP S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN SAP Analytics Cloud Workday
