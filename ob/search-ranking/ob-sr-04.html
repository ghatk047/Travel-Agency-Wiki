OB-SR-04

Package Search and Dynamic Bundling

The process document for OB-SR-04 outlines the steps involved in package search and dynamic bundling, ensuring personalized travel packages are presented to users based on their preferences and search criteria.

OB — OTA Online Booking and Search Search Ranking and Personalisation

Process Overview

Trigger User initiates a search query through Expedia Open World or Partner Central.
Outcome A personalized package bundle is dynamically created and displayed to the user, enhancing the booking experience with relevant offers and deals.
Systems Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport SAP Concur T2 SAP Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Tableau
