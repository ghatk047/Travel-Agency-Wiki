SP-AC-01

Car Rental API Integration Hertz Avis Enterprise

This process document outlines the integration of Hertz and Avis car rental APIs with Expedia Group's OTA systems and Amex GBT / Concur TMC systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Activities Cars and Ancillaries

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new partnership or update in API specifications between Expedia Group and Hertz/Avis.
Outcome Successful integration of Hertz and Avis car rental APIs, enabling seamless booking and management within Expedia Group's OTA and TMC platforms.
