SP-AC-03

Travel Insurance Product Integration and Management

This process document outlines the integration and management of travel insurance products across various OTA and TMC systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Activities Cars and Ancillaries

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new travel insurance product or update to an existing one by the Expedia Group or Amex GBT / SAP Concur teams.
Outcome Successful integration and management of the travel insurance product, ensuring seamless availability and processing within all relevant systems.
Systems Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau SAP Concur T2 SAP Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete SAP S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN SAP Analytics Cloud Workday
