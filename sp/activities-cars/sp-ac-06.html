SP-AC-06

Ancillary Product Pricing and Margin Management

This process document outlines the steps for managing ancillary product pricing and margin management across various OTA (Online Travel Agency) and TMC (Travel Management Company) systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Activities Cars and Ancillaries

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new pricing strategy or update to existing ancillary products by the business team.
Outcome Successful implementation of updated ancillary product pricing and margin management, ensuring alignment with business goals and compliance with regulatory requirements.
