SP-AI-03

Low-Cost Carrier Direct Integration

This process document outlines the integration of Low-Cost Carrier (LCC) direct feeds into OTA and TMC systems for enhanced inventory management and booking capabilities.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Airline and Transport Supply

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new LCC partnership or update in existing LCC data feed requirements
Outcome Successful integration of LCC direct feeds, enabling real-time inventory updates and streamlined booking processes across Expedia Group's OTA and Amex GBT/Concur TMC systems.
Systems AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds Concur T2 Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete Amadeus GDS S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday
