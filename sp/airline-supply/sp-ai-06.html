SP-AI-06

Schedule Change and Involuntary Re-booking Management

This process document outlines the steps for managing schedule changes and involuntary re-bookings between OTA systems and TMC systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Airline and Transport Supply

Process Overview

Trigger Notification of a schedule change or involuntary re-booking from an airline supplier.
Outcome Successful communication and execution of the schedule change or re-booking across all relevant systems, ensuring minimal disruption to travelers and efficient use of resources.
