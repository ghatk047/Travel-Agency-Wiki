SP-HT-02

Rate and Availability Loading and Synchronisation

This process document outlines the steps for loading and synchronizing hotel rates and availability across various OTA and TMC systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Hotel and Accommodation Supply

Process Overview

Trigger Daily scheduled job or manual initiation by an administrator
Outcome Successful synchronization of hotel rates and availability data across all integrated systems, ensuring consistent pricing and inventory information for travelers.
