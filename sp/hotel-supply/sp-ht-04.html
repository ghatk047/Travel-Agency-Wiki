SP-HT-04

Rate Parity Monitoring and Enforcement

This process document outlines the Rate Parity Monitoring and Enforcement procedure for Expedia Group OTA and Amex GBT / Concur TMC systems.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Hotel and Accommodation Supply

Process Overview

Trigger Daily automated rate comparison between OTA and TMC systems.
Outcome Ensuring consistent pricing across all platforms, minimizing discrepancies, and maintaining supplier relationships.
Systems AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau Concur T2 Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete Amadeus GDS S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday
