SP-HT-06

Commission and Merchant Model Configuration

This process document outlines the configuration of commission and merchant models for hotel and accommodation supply within the OTA Supplier and Inventory Management domain.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Hotel and Accommodation Supply

Process Overview

Trigger A request from a supplier or internal stakeholder to configure a new commission or merchant model.
Outcome Successful configuration of the commission and merchant model, ensuring accurate revenue sharing and cost management for both suppliers and travel companies.
