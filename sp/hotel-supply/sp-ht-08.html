SP-HT-08

Supplier Contract Negotiation and Renewal

The process document outlines the steps for negotiating and renewing supplier contracts within the OTA Supplier and Inventory Management domain, focusing on hotel and accommodation supply.

SP — OTA Supplier and Inventory Management Hotel and Accommodation Supply

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of contract negotiation or renewal based on contract expiration dates or performance reviews.
Outcome Successful negotiation and renewal of supplier contracts with improved terms and conditions that align with business goals and market trends.
