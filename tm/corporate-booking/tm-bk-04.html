TM-BK-04

Booking Change and Re-booking Schedule Changes IROPS

This process document outlines the steps for handling booking changes and re-booking schedule changes within the TMC Travel Programme Management domain, specifically focusing on corporate booking and servicing.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Corporate Booking and Servicing

Process Overview

Trigger A request for a booking change or re-booking is initiated by a traveler through the Concur T2 system.
Outcome The booking change or re-booking is successfully processed, and all relevant parties are notified of the update.
Systems Concur T2 Concur Expense Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds AWS SageMaker Snowflake Kafka Salesforce Tableau S/4HANA International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday
