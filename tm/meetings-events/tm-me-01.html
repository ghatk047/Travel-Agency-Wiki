TM-ME-01

Meeting Request Intake and RFP Management

This process document outlines the steps for managing meeting request intake and RFP (Request for Proposal) management within the TMC Travel Programme Management domain, focusing on Meetings Events and MICE.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Meetings Events and MICE

Process Overview

Trigger A business unit submits a meeting request through the SAP Concur T2 system.
Outcome The meeting request is successfully processed, an RFP is generated if necessary, and the best provider is selected based on the evaluation criteria.
Systems SAP Concur T2 SAP S/4HANA Cvent Email System
