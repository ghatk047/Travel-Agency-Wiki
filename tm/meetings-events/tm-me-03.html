TM-ME-03

Event Registration and Attendee Management Cvent

This process document outlines the event registration and attendee management workflow using Cvent, focusing on seamless integration with TMC systems for efficient meeting event management.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Meetings Events and MICE

Process Overview

Trigger Event creation or update in Cvent by a Meeting Event Coordinator (MEC)
Outcome Successful registration of attendees, automated communication, expense tracking, and compliance with travel policies.
Systems Cvent Concur T2 Concur Expense S/4HANA Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport Analytics Cloud Tableau
