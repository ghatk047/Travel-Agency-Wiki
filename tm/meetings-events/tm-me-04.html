TM-ME-04

Air and Ground Transportation Coordination

This process document outlines the coordination of air and ground transportation for meetings, events, and MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) programs managed by Expedia Group's OTA systems and Amex GBT / Concur TMC.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Meetings Events and MICE

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new meeting or event requiring transportation services
Outcome Successful coordination and booking of air and ground transportation for all attendees, ensuring timely arrival and departure with minimal disruptions.
