TM-ME-07

Virtual and Hybrid Meeting Technology Setup

This process document outlines the steps for setting up virtual and hybrid meeting technology, ensuring seamless integration between OTA and TMC systems.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Meetings Events and MICE

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new virtual or hybrid meeting by the travel team
Outcome Successful setup and execution of the virtual or hybrid meeting with all necessary technologies integrated and functioning properly.
