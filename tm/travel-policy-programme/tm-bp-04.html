TM-BP-04

Preferred Supplier Rate Loading Airlines Hotels Cars

This process document outlines the steps for loading preferred supplier rates for airlines, hotels, and cars into the TMC systems.

TM — TMC Travel Programme Management Business Travel Policy and Programme

Process Overview

Trigger Initiation of a new business travel policy or update to existing preferred suppliers.
Outcome Successful loading of preferred supplier rates into all relevant TMC and OTA systems, ensuring accurate pricing and availability for travelers.
Systems Concur T2 AWS SageMaker Snowflake Tableau Kafka Expedia Open World Partner Central Amadeus NDC Sabre GDS Travelport EVC One Key TravelAds Concur Expense Amex GBT Neo/Complete International SOS Cvent Amex Corporate Card VCN Analytics Cloud Workday Salesforce
